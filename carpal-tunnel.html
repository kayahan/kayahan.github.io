Peripheral Nerves

We offer comprehensive neurosurgery, neurological treatments and surgical options for peripheral nerve disorders and injuries. Carpal tunnel syndrome is the most common peripheral nerve disorder, but others are possible. Schedule a visit to our office to learn about options to help eliminate pain and regain your mobility.

CARPAL TUNNEL SYNDROME AND PERIPHERAL NERVES

  • Carpal Tunnel Release
  • Ulnar Decompression
  • Tarsal Tunnel
  • Sciatic Nerve and its Branches
  • Peripheral Nerve Tumor Surgery
  • Nerve Biopsy
  • Occipital and other Neurectomies

OTHER SURGERIES

  • Vagal Nerve Stimulation
  • Carotid Endarterectomy
  • Muscle Biopsy
