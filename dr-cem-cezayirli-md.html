Cem Cezayirli, M.D.

Dr. Cezayirili specializes in spine and brain surgery to correct injuries and birth defects to help patients regain control over their life. He took over the clinic over 12 years ago when longtime Birmingham surgeonDr. Haynes retired and turned his practice over to Dr. Cezayirili.
